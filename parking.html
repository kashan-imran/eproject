Parking At The Nexus Aquarium

Parking is available to guests of nexus aquarium in the City of washington parking lot located north of the Aquarium off the east side of the circle at Channelside Drive.

Designated accessible parking is available on a first-come, first-serve basis.

Payment can be made via the pay stations throughout the lot or via the ParkMobile, Passport, or PaybyPhone mobile apps.

Member Parking


Aquarium members enjoy free parking on a first-come, first-service basis, but must register their
vehicle(s) to receive this benefit
