Educators

For Educators

Nexus Aquarium school programs provide opportunities for students to discover the aquatic realm in an immersive, standards-based learning environment. Students discover conservation efforts and connect with nature through one of our onsite programs: Education General Admission (self exploration), Instructor-led programming and Tuesdays in the Field OR bring Georgia Aquarium to your school through in person programs: Outreach or our virtual programs: Field Trips (pre-recorded lessons) or Outreach (instructor led lesson).




Career Day 2023!

NExus Aquarium Education department is otter-ly excited to announce the return of our Career Day, Thursday, March
2nd from 10am to 1pm.

All schools that book a field trip for the day are invited to speak with Aquarium professionals as well as local professionals from a variety of different fields such as Environmental Health, Research and Conservation, Veterinarians and many more. Dive deep into the educational requirements, special training, and personal stories from all the professionals about their careers. Give your students the opportunity to discover an ocean of possibilities!

Outreach

Nexus Aquarium has an extensive outreach program, from animal encounters to biofacts and learning games. Your students will have an opportunity to engage with an Aquarium instructor from the comfort and safety of their program environment.

Nexus Aquarium is proud to be a participant in the annual Great American Teach-In held each November.
