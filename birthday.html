Birthday Party

Party with the Fishes

The Florida Aquarium welcomes children ages 1-12 to have a fin-tastic birthday celebration with us! With five unique party spaces to choose from (both indoor and outdoor), a birthday at the Aquarium is sure to make a splash! Birthday parties are held on Saturdays and Sundays, either

10AM – 12PM or 1:30PM – 3:30PM.

Aquarium members receive discounted party packages.

All parties include:

  • Two hours of party time in a private party space
  • Aquarium party host
  • Visit from one of the Aquarium’s animal ambassadors
  • Delicious cake from Publix
  • Water and lemonade
  • Special gift for the birthday child
  • Digital party invitation
  • Aquatic-themed decorations
  • Party supplies (cups, napkins, plates, forks, candles)
  • Admission to the Aquarium for all guests

Rooms

Coastal Room – maximum 30 guests

Green Room – maximum 30 guests

Open Ocean Room – maximum 30 guests

Backyard Room (designed for children ages 1-5) – maximum 20 guests

Choose your Activity

Treasure Quest (all ages): Take a journey with an aquarium scavenger hunt.

Creature Challenge (ages 4-12): Engage in a fun animal game!

Put it to the Test (ages 4-12): Tap into your inner scientist with exciting and interactive science experiments.

Bones About It (all ages): Learn all about sharks, turtles, or shells (your choice).

Food & Beverage

No outside food is permitted. Water, lemonade and a sheet cake from Publix are included in each party package. Additional food and beverage can be added and must be ordered and paid in full 10 days prior to your party.

You may bring in your own cake; however, no discount will be applied to the party package price. An additional $20 cake handling fee will be added to your reservation.

Leftover food and beverage may not be taken home (with the exception of the Publix cake).

A quote will be provided at the time of reservation. Prices are subject to change without notice.

Contact Reservations directly if the birthday child or any guests have food allergies.

Decorations

Aquatic-themed decorations including centerpieces, tablecloths and cake display are provided. Additional table decorations and party favors may be supplied by the party host; however, all decorations must be approved by Nexus Aquarium.

To ensure the safety of our animals and in alignment with our mission of conservation, Nexus Aquarium has a strict No Balloon Policy. No balloons or inflatables will be permitted.

Please contact Reservations for more information or with any questions or concerns.
